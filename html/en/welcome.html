Furnished Tourism Rental, Barr (Alsace)

Charming apartment in an old Alsatian half-timbered house in the heart of the city centre of Barr, Alsace (France).

20 minutes from Strasbourg, Bas-Rhin, on the wine road of Alsace, in the Vosges, charming apartment in an old Alsatian half-timbered house in the heart of the city center of the small community of Barr.

Beamed ceilings and ancient furniture give the place a typical charm.

Magnificent views of the vineyards of the area from the living room, and the Castle of Haut-Andlau and the city itself from the bedroom windows.
